Our Dairy Farm Story

Our Aim: Purity and Wellness

At Sais Farms, our mission is simple: to provide the purest, most nutritious dairy products while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and sustainable farming. We believe healthy cows produce truly healthy milk.

What We Provide

We specialize in ethically-sourced, grass-fed A2 cow's milk. From this exceptional milk, we craft:

  • Farm Fresh Milk: Raw, unpasteurized, and delivered within hours of milking.
  • Traditional Curd: Naturally set, rich in probiotics, and slow-cultured.
  • A2 Ghee: Made with the slow Bilona process for maximum flavor and nutrition.
